ICC T20 World Cup 2024 Analysis: Player Performance & Impact Analytics

Problem Statement
In T20 cricket, player performance is often judged using basic metrics like runs and wickets. However, these do not fully capture a player's true impact on match outcomes.
This project analyzes match and player data from the ICC T20 World Cup 2024 to identify high-impact players, evaluate consistency, and uncover patterns that influence team performance.

Objectives
* Analyze player performance across 50+ matches
* Identify high-impact and consistent performers
* Compare team strategies and performance trends
* Build a data-driven approach to player evaluation

Dashboard Preview

Key Insights
* Top-order batters contributed majority of runs → Early momentum plays a critical role in match outcomes
* Strike rate > 140 strongly correlates with match-winning performances
* Certain players show high consistency despite low total runs → Reliable contributors under pressure
* Teams with balanced bowling attacks (pace + spin) performed better across conditions
* Death-over performance significantly impacts match results → Both batting and bowling phases critical
* High-variance players contribute match-winning performances but lack consistency

Recommendations
* Prioritize players with **high strike rate + consistency, not just total runs
* Focus on balanced team composition (batting depth + bowling variety)
* Strengthen death-over strategies for both batting and bowling
* Use data-driven metrics for player selection and match planning

Tools & Technologies
* Power BI – Dashboarding & Visualization
* DAX – KPI creation & calculations

Key Features
* Interactive dashboard for player and team analysis
* KPI-based performance comparison
* Match-wise and player-wise trend analysis
* Data-driven insights for strategic decision-making

What I Learned

* Applying data storytelling in sports analytics
* Building dashboards focused on decision-making, not just visualization
